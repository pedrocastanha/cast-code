# Brand Voice

Use this skill when copy needs to sound consistent with a company or product voice.

## Voice System

- Extract recurring vocabulary, banned phrases, tone, pacing, and proof style.
- Maintain a short voice guide before rewriting large assets.
- Preserve factual claims; improve clarity without inventing evidence.
- Match voice to channel: ads are compressed, emails are sequenced, landing pages need proof.

## Review Checklist

- The copy names the audience and the promised outcome.
- The tone stays consistent across headline, body, CTA, and objection handling.
- Claims are specific enough to be testable.
- Risky legal, pricing, or competitive claims are called out before publishing.
