# Marketing Campaigns

Use this skill when the task is to plan, audit, or improve a campaign.

## Campaign Brief Structure

- Audience: define segment, pain, trigger, buying committee, and awareness stage.
- Positioning: one core promise, why now, proof points, objections, and differentiators.
- Offer: make the conversion action explicit and realistic for the channel.
- Channels: map creative format, targeting intent, landing destination, and measurement.
- Metrics: name leading indicators, conversion metrics, cost metrics, and review cadence.

## Quality Bar

- Prefer concrete campaign decisions over generic marketing advice.
- Tie each message to a buyer pain and a product capability.
- Separate tests by hypothesis, audience, channel, and creative variable.
- Flag any action that spends budget, publishes externally, or mutates an ad account.
