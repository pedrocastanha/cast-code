# Campaign Brief Fixture

## Product

Cast Benchmark Lab helps teams evaluate agent workflows before changing prompts, models, connectors, or automation schedules.

## Campaign Goal

Drive qualified trials from teams that already run AI coding agents and want a private benchmark loop.

## Required Output Sections

- Audience
- Positioning
- Channel plan
- Quality benchmark plan
- Cost and latency reporting
- Privacy and approval policy

## Constraints

- Do not publish ads or mutate external accounts during a dry run.
- Use local fixtures and redacted artifacts in automated tests.
- Escalate only when a benchmark requires writing files or touching external systems.
