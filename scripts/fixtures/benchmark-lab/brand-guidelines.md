# Cast Benchmark Lab Brand Guidelines

## Positioning

Cast is a private agent workspace for teams that want local-first automation, project memory, domain environments, and measurable agent quality.

## Voice

- Direct, technical, and useful.
- Prefer concrete operational detail over hype.
- Show privacy boundaries explicitly when describing platform sync.
- Treat benchmark evidence as a first-class product surface.

## Audience

- Engineering teams evaluating model changes.
- Marketing operators building repeatable campaign workflows.
- Design teams turning Figma context into implementation tasks.
- Founders who need private local automation with optional platform management.

## Campaign Guardrails

- Every campaign brief must include audience, positioning, channels, metrics, and privacy notes.
- Paid media work must default to read-only analysis unless an explicit approval gate exists.
- Benchmarks should compare quality, latency, reliability, and cost.
